###Reviewer #3:

This manuscript reports on a proton conducting solid oxide fuel cells with La0.7Sr0.3Mn0.8M0.2O3- (M=Mn, Mg) as cathode to remove the nitrogen oxides. The authors provide convincing evidence using polarization resistance, NOx conversions and catalytic activities to verify the good performance of as-prepared cathode. Authors have used a number of techniques to describe the works on well characterized. However, in my opinion, there are many problems in the manuscript. Except English writing, the novelty of this work reported here cannot achieve the high-quality requirements of Journal of Power Sources. So, I think this manuscript cannot be accepted due to the following questions:

##审稿意见

  1. Page 7, Line 36: ‘’the metal nitrates La(NO3)3, Mn(Ac)2, Sr(NO3)2, Mg(NO3)2 were dissolved in deionized water’’. Mn(Ac)2 is not metal nitrate.

删掉metal nitrates

  1. Page 8, Line 46-47: ‘’And BZCY-LSMM (or LSM) composite cathodes were brushed-painted on the BZCY electrolyte and subsequently fired at 1100°C for 2h in air to form a porous cathode layer’’. In this part, BZCY-LSMM composite cathode was used as cathode. However, in 3.2 part, the cell performance of H-SOFC was conducted on a LSMM or LSM single cathode.

这个在下文是简写，并没有事实错误。
阴极一直都是（BZCY+LSMM）。下文阴极写LSMM只是突出重点。
修改加了一句
The BZCY-LSMM composite cathodes are abbreviated as as LSMM and BZCY-LSM as LSM. And NiO+BZCY is abbreviated as NIO. That is to say, NIO+BZCY/BZCY/LSMM+BZCY equals to NIO/BZCY/LSMM.

  1. Page10, Line 13-14: ‘’LSMM and BZCY with the ratio of 4:1 were mechanically mixed and calcined at temperature 1000°C for 3h’’. In the experimental part, the cathode slurry was brushed-painted on the BZCY electrolyte and calcined at 1100°C for 2h. So, in order to test the chemical compatibility of BZCY and LSMM, the mixture should be calcined at 1100°C rather than 1000°C.

这块的确是有个设计缺陷。 当时想的是工作温度（最大800）下是稳定的。
这个1100 重烧测一下ok。

  1. Page 11, Line 31-36: ‘’the polarization impedance (Rp) decreases obviously with the rise of NOx concentration from 1000ppm to 20000 ppm (2w ppm), which indicates NO does react at the cathode’’. (1) The polarization impedance does not decrease obviously with the rise of NOx concentration at 600°C. Authors should provide corresponding explanations. (2) Except for the NOx concentration of 5000 ppm, the impedance spectra of LSM obtained at 800°C with the different NOx concentration all can be divided into two arcs obviously. Authors should provide corresponding explanations. (3) Why the impedance spectra of LSMM exhibit a linear relationship at 700°C and 650°C with the NOx concentration of 1w ppm and 5000 ppm?

1

  1. Page 14, Line 29: ‘’LSM/BZCY/NiO’’ and ‘’LSMM/BZCY/NiO’’ should be ‘’LSM/BZCY/NiO-BZCY’’ and ‘’LSMM/BZCY/NiO-BZCY’’

这个同上

  1. Page 14: the impedance spectra of LSM/BZCY/NiO-BZCY and LSMM/BZCY/NiO-BZCY cells should be presented.

全电池阻抗谱，如若必要，可加在附录中。当时由于图数目有限制，就没加。

  1. Page 15: Authors should make possible explanations that why the NOx conversion of LSMM is lower than that of LSM at 800°C.

这个800℃两个差距不大，我认为没有啥可解释的

  1. Page 17, Fig. 6(c): Is the LSM sample has a larger desorption peak at around 850°C?

850不是工作温度范围，此外仪器极限是1000，已经测到900了

  1. English must be improved by native English speakers or professional English editing companies.
